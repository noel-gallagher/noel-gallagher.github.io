About me

I'm a software engineer passionate about building robust, scalable, and performant systems. I enjoy looking under the hood of the technologies I use and am always seeking opportunities understand the fundamentals and trade-offs of implementations.

As you can probably tell from my blog, I spend a lot of my time thinking about and experimenting with concepts related to distributed computing and databases.

This blog is where I document my learnings, share my notes, and connect with others who are passionate about these topics. If you find this content interesting or have any questions, feel free to reach out to me. let's connect!
